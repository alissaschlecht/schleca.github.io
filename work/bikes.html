JUST FOR FUN

Bike Builder

Overview

Why

Throwback! Here's my first project using Illustrator AND AngularJS

It's perhaps not the most intuitive process, but it's about bikes, which in my 100% subjective opinion overrides some of it's usability issues. Just like a great UI can make up for a small amout of bad UX (Aesthetic-Usability Effect).

This project is also significant because it marks the moment when I realized that I really, truly wanted to dive into UX. I knew that there were some things that weren't working, but I didn't have the vocabulary, visual library, and general knowledge to put my finger on exactly WHY it wasn't working.

I would love to re-do this project. I know it's better to have done it already, but I haven't quite yet. In the meantime, feel free to check it out in it's current form

Roadmap

The future is here

I completed this project in July 2018. Presto has just launched (January 2019) an app for the card! It has very similar functionality to what I discovered and outlined for this app, so it's great to see that I might have been on the right track. You can see the new app here (Apple).
